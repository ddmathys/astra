About ASTRA

ASTRA consolidates stock market investments, including both standard markets (ETFs and stocks) and alternative markets (cryptocurrency). It allows users to enter various types of investment transactions such as purchases, sales, and dividends.

Key Features:

  • Investment Tracking: Users can input detailed information about their investments at the time of transaction, including type (buy/sell) and dividends.
  • Performance Overview: ASTRA provides an up-to-date view of the overall performance of the user's investments.
  • Dividend Tracking: Users can see the dividends received each year, helping them to understand their income from investments.
  • Asset Allocation: The app displays the distribution of investments across different asset classes.
  • Individual Performance: Users can view the performance of each individual investment.
  • Community Insights: Users can see insights from others using the same application and share their own opinions on various investments.

ASTRA aims to provide a comprehensive tool for investors to manage and analyze their portfolios effectively.
